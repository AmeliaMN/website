A 3-D modeling software like Cinema 4D or Google SketchUp can allow you to visualize more realistic objects

While at the University of Cincinnati, I learned how to use the 3D modeling software Cinema 4D (we also worked briefly with Google's SketchUp program). Below are a few examples of projects I created in class, one from life and one a conceptual design.

A lamp study from life

This lamp was rendered from life. Sketches were first made on paper, and the render was developed from there.

A conceptual chair study

This chair was developed on paper, in Adobe Illustrator, and finally rendered in Cinema 4D.
