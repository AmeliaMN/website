Standard Story

You will be writing an approximately 500-word-long “Standard Story,” using Sarah Cohen’s list. This means you could choose

  • a budget, like the Massachusetts state budget or the Smith budget
  • a weather event, like the supposedly forthcoming storm
  • a ranking, like the US News and World Report College Rankings or the TIOBE programming language rankings
  • another standard story you vett with me!

As always, I’d like your story to include all the standard journalistic elements:

  • Headline
  • Byline
  • Lede (soft or hard)
  • Nutgraph
  • Exposition
  • Conclusion, which hopefully wraps back around to the beginning of your story
  • Word count

Considerations:

  • Accuracy
  • Context of data
  • Spelling and grammar
  • Clarity of writing
